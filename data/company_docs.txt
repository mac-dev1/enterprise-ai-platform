Onboarding process:
All new employees must complete HR documentation, attend orientation, and receive system access within the first week.

IT Support:
For technical issues, employees must submit a ticket through the internal system. Critical issues are handled within 24 hours.

Vacation Policy:
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave per year. Requests must be approved by managers.

Security Policy:
Passwords must be updated every 90 days. Sensitive data must not be shared externally.
